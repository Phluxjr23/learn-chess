## basics
this is the main play area for chess. 
i want you to go ahead and open [this link](https://lichess.org/editor/8/8/8/8/8/8/8/8_w_-_-_0_1?color=white&position=518) in your web browser. this is an empty lichess board, that we'll set up and look at together!
as you can see, it is an 8x8 board with light and dark squares. ignore everything else for now btw, **we'll get to that later.**
you can **also** see that there's white and black pieces. these are the two teams of chess! as you already know, your main goal is to checkmate the other player.

now that we've analyzed the board, lets set it up!
first, look at this board:
![a chess board with the kings highlighed for clarity.](https://images.chesscomfiles.com/uploads/v1/images_users/tiny_mce/ColinStapczynski/phpoODwO2.png)
